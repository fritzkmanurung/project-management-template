Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new project?

To create a new project, navigate to the Projects page and click the "New Project" button. Fill in the required details such as project name, description, team, priority, and dates. Once completed, click "Create Project" to save.

How do I add team members to a project?

Navigate to the Team page and click the "Invite Member" button. Enter their email address and assign appropriate permissions. They will receive an invitation to join your project.

What is the Kanban board used for?

The Kanban board helps you visualize your project workflow. Tasks move through different stages (To Do, In Progress, Review, Done) as they progress. You can add new tasks, assign them to team members, and track progress in real-time.

How do I view project reports?

Go to the Reports page to access comprehensive analytics and insights. You can filter by date range, project, team, or status. Reports include task completion rates, team performance, and project progress charts.

Can I customize my notification settings?

Yes! Navigate to Settings and scroll to the Notifications section. You can toggle email notifications, push notifications, task reminders, and project updates according to your preferences.

How do I switch between light and dark mode?

Click the moon/sun icon in the header to toggle between light and dark mode. Your preference will be saved automatically. You can also set it to follow your system preference in Settings.
